Our mission

BackupVerified exists to close the gap between “we have backups” and “we can recover.” Backups are essential, but without verification they can fail silently — until the worst possible moment.

Build confidence

Provide clear signals that backups exist, are recent, and can restore successfully.

Keep it secure

Encrypt backups before upload and avoid collecting sensitive secrets through forms.

Respect ownership

Store backups in your AWS S3 bucket so you stay in control of your data.

Backups should be provable

Many teams only discover a backup problem during an emergency restore. Our approach is to test restores regularly, track outcomes, and make recovery a routine — not a gamble.
